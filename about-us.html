About Twilite - Jim Murray - Photographer

Jim Murray is the owner and founder of Twilite Real Estate Photography in Raleigh, NC. He has been a professional photographer in North Carolina for four years and specializes in residential and commercial real estate in the Triangle area. Jim also has additional experience with weddings and nature photography, portrait and landscape images, and blue and golden light photoshoots to complement each style.

Jim is passionate about the combined art and science of photography, from infinite lighting variations to the camera equipment itself. He enjoys taking that knowledge and collaborating with clients to find ways to capture and accentuate their home, realize their vision and help them build their business as a result.

In his free time, Jim enjoys going on adventures with this grandson, visiting the beach, running and going to concerts.
